Our Staff

Steven A. Davis, M.D.

Director and Principal Investigator

The center’s founder and principal investigator, got his B.A. in Economics from Yale University and his M.D. from the University of Texas Southwestern Medical School at Dallas. Dr. Davis completed his dermatology residency at the University of California San Francisco Medical School, after which he was on the academic faculty there for six years. He was certified by the American Board of Dermatology in 1977. Dr. Davis has over 20 years of experience doing clinical research studies.

He has published in the lay and scientific press and is currently Clinical Professor of Medicine (Dermatology) at the University of Texas Health Science Center at San Antonio. In additional to his dermatology practice – the Dermatology & Laser Center of San Antonio – Dr. Davis serves on the Executive Board of BioMedSA, (see BioMedSA.org) and the Foundation Board of the South Texas Blood & Tissue Center. Dr. Davis also has an extensive background in media experience, including 33 continuous years as the producer and voice of “Speaking of Health” an award-winning feature on the CBS Radio Network.

Gloria J. Johnson, PA-C MPAS

Sub-Investigator

Served in the US Air Force for 20 years and received a Bachelor of Science, Physician Associate, from the University of Oklahoma in 1985. She did an Otolaryngology/Head and Neck Surgery PA Residency in 1993. She received a Master's in Physician Assistant Studies from the University of Nebraska in 2000. She has a very diverse clinical background which includes experience in family practice, primary care/urgent care, otolaryngology, neurology, and dermatology and is national certified as a Physician Assistant.

Ross Davis, MPAS, PA-C

Sub-Investigator

Ross Davis, MPAS, PA-C, earned his Master of Science in Physican Assistant Studies degree from Pacific University in Forest Grove, Oregon and is certified by the National Commission on Certification of Physician Assistants. He is licensed by the Texas State Board of Medical Examiners. He is also a member of the Society of Dermatology Physician Assistants, American Academy of Physician Assistants, and Texas Academy of Physician Assistants.

Specialty-trained in dermatology, Mr. Davis is qualified to perform a comprehensive dermatological examination, diagnose, treat, and prescribe appropriate medical treatment for a broad range of dermatological needs in both adults and children. When not working, Ross enjoys studying jazz improvisation, hiking, and traveling to experience different cultures.

Janice K. Orem, CMA

Research Coordinator

graduated from San Antonio College with an Associate of Arts in Business Technology in May 1975. In 1977, she became a Certified Medical Assistant. From 1976 to 2007 she was the Business Manager for a busy Urology Practice with 5 physicians and 17 ancillary personnel.

She has been involved with clinical research since 1994 and joined the Dermatology Clinical Research Center of San Antonio in January 2008. She is a member of the following organizations: San Antonio Medical Managers, Medical Group Management Association, American Medical Assistants, Bexar County Medical Assistants, and Association of Clinical Research Professionals.

Janice is a very active member of the San Antonio community and does volunteer work for her church and several nursing homes.

Judy Lochte

Research Coordinator

has been in clinical research for over 15 years. Her background includes experience as a medical assistant in a large Urology and clinical research practice prior to joining the Dermatology and Clinical Research Center of San Antonio in April of 2013. Judy is versed in every phase of clinical trials, laboratory procedures, patient recruitment and retention.

Judy is a dedicated member of her church, does volunteer work and in her spare time, has made many beautiful quilts
